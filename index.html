What is The Cambridge Vault?

TheCambridgeVault is an independent project that serves to bring memories and nostalgia to the graduating class of 2016. The aim is to create a friendly and enjoyable environment for the class of 2016 to share any memories, photos, videos or experiences that can be accessed at any time.
